Lasagna

Description

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 pack of lasagna pasta
  • 250g of ground beef
  • 2 mozzarella balls
  • 2 small onions
  • 1L of milk
  • 2 Tbsp of butter
  • 2 Tbsp of corn flour
  • spices
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • thyme
  • garlic

Steps

  1. cut and mix the tomatoes with olive oil
  2. Heat the pan over medium heat before adding oil then the mixed tomatoes
  3. add speices and 1 tablespoon tomato paste (optional)
  4. let it cook uncovered
  5. Finely chop the onions, and let caramelise over medium heated oiled pan with garlic and salt
  6. Add beef and cover
  7. In a saucepan, over low heat let butter melt (Do not boil), then add corn flour and mix
  8. Add the milk gradually and stir until boiling
  9. boil the lasagna pasta sheets 1 - 2 mins
  10. Grease the baking dish and assemble the lasagna; starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella
  11. Repeat 3 - 4 times
  12. Bake at 180Â°C for 40 mins
